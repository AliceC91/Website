Why should I join?

We pride ourselves in the community we built throughout the years. What makes us unique is not the different disciplines we teach, but rather, the family we have created.

 

Our students encourage each other through sessions and keep in contact even after classes have finished. The community we have here in Piki’s Dojo enables us to organise social events to include those in the neighbourhood.

 

We are not just a dojo, we are a place for people of all walks of life to come together and share something they all have in common - passion of martial arts and so we welcome you to be part of our little club.

How much would it cost me?

 

We believe that fitness and well-being shouldn’t need to break the bank, so we’ve negotiated a fee that is manageable to everyone.

 

If you want to pay per class, each class is £1, where each class is 2h long. We have a concession charge is £0.50 and this applies to members, children under 16 years, students (with a valid student card) and the elderly (>65).

 

To join obtain membership, there is an annual fee of £20 which is normally paid in January.
