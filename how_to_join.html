How to join Piki's in Belfast

 

How can I join - Joining us is easy, you can come into the dojo in person, we have a receptionist manning the counter from 09.00-18.00 on Mondays to Fridays, and 09.00 to 16.00 on Saturdays.

 

The Dojo is still open on Sunday but there will be no receptionist and fewer staff. Feel free to come in for quiet meditation or to practise alone or with friends.

 

You can also call us on 02890000000 and provide details over the phone, or send in an email to pikidojo@gmail.com.

 

If you want, we also take snail mail to the following address: 22 Cheese Road Belfast BT5 9HJ. In all cases, you can request a free taster session of either discipline and we will introduce you to the master in charge.
