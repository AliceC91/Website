Namaste

To be recognised as an institute that produces students with knowledge behind the history of marital arts, shapes great technique, and builds on personality as they progress through the ranks. Our institute not only inspires young minds to open their creativity through different forms, it also teaches them to harness their inner chakra through meditation and quiet reflection.

We welcome you to come and visit our dojo and experience the atmosphere first-hand for children as young as 3 all the way through to adults aged 100. Our classes are tailored for the needs of our students. Naturally this means the course structure changes with the requirements of the students.

“Pikachi’s Dojo has made my life so much better, I now see life in a more positive and optimistic light. You might think it’s just a wee exercise club but the life lessons I get from the teachings of my masters are invaluable.” - Bruce Lee, 32

“Coming here is the best thing on Tuesdays” - William Craig, 24

We focus on a holistic approach to martial arts and though not a goal, most of our students, young or young at heart, go on to compete in major international competitions. The nature of our institution fosters an environment of self-improvement and mindfulness and this is what makes our institute unique.
